## What to try

**1. Quick Lookup (Dashboard — 15 seconds)**
Sign in → Dashboard → type any ADGM regulatory question → "Look up →"
Try: "What is a Category 3A licence?"
Click the gold [COBS 23.12.2] citation chip to see the exact rule text.

**2. Deep Research (Research page — 90 seconds)**
Click "Research" in sidebar → use an example query or type your own.
The 10-agent pipeline runs in full. Results include a structured
research note with grounded claims and confidence tiers.
Export the result as Word or PDF.

**3. Regulatory Monitoring (Product Twins)**
Click "Product twins" → open TradeDar Ltd → "Run assessment"
See how QANUN tracks a product structure against the live corpus.

**4. Corpus Browse**
Click "Browse corpus" → search any regulatory term.
63,397 provisions across ADGM, DIFC, and El Salvador.
